About Frida

Magdalena Carmen Frida Kahlo y Calderón was a Mexican painter known for her many portraits, self-portraits, and works inspired by the nature and artifacts of Mexico.

Curiosities

  • Her nickname was Peg Leg.
  • The artist was a polyglot.
  • She had a car accident at 18 that prevented her from being a mother.
  • He wrote a note before he died.
